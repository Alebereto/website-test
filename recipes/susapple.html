Susapple

Description

The susapple is much like an apple, except it is undoubtedly suspicious, which is where it gets its name from - the sus-apple.

Many believe that the susapple grows from a susapple tree, however this is false. the susapple is a product of fusing the sus gene with an ordinary apple.

Ingredients:

  • apple
  • suspicious creature

Steps:

  1. pick an apple
  2. infuse the apple with the suspicious creature's gene
  3. wait 2-124 minutes
