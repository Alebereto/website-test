Mongpie

Description

The Mongpie, or the Among us pie as it is sometimes reffered to, is just like a regular pie, except the pie kind of looks like it contains an among us character on its surface.

The Mongpie comes in many forms, but for this recipe we are going with the classic take of the Mongpie.

Ingredients:

  • pie
  • among us character drawing

Steps:

  1. obtain a pie
  2. make an among us on the pie
